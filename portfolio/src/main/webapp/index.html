Intro

Hi! My name is Julia and I am a rising junior at Barnard College of Columbia University. I am majoring in computer science and minoring in economics. I am interested in the intersection between technology and business.


On campus, I hold leadership positions in several clubs:

  • I am currently the President of the Columbia chapter of the TAMID Group a pre-professional club, with 57 chapters across different college campuses, that offers experiential business and consulting opportunities for undergraduates. Students partner with real companies and start-ups to gain hands-on-experience. This upcoming fall, we are planning to pilot a third track, a technology track.
  • I am currently the Executive Vice President and in the process of co-founding a chapter of The Women's Network, a national women-led networking organization focused on connecting ambitious undergraduate students with professional workers.
